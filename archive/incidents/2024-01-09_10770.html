Description

Two skiers were skiing individually down the northwest-facing slope of the Steinmandl when the skier in front triggered a small avalanche. Based on the tracks, the skier in front was likely carried approximately 30 meters but not buried. The two skiers then continued skiing towards the Gerachsattel. A witness, who observed this from the Hählekopf and believed one person was buried, reported the incident to the emergency services.

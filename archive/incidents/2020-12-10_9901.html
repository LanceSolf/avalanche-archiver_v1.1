Description

Two ski tourers were traversing the Ifen basin towards the Hoher Ifen when they triggered an avalanche. One of them was swept approximately 30 meters but was not buried. No one was injured. Both then continued their tour. Source: PI Kleinwalsertal and surveys by the LWK Kleinwalsertal – see also snow profile from December 11, 2020; Photo: LWD Pecl

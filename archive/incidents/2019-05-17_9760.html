Großer Widderstein / Karlstor

2019-05-17 16:00:00

Date: 2019-05-17 16:00:00
Location: Großer Widderstein / Karlstor
Elevation: N/A
Incline: N/A
Aspect: N/A
Coordinates: 47.28666, 10.13085

Description

A wet loose snow avalanche, which started in a rocky gully near the Widderstein summit, grew larger in the cirque below the Karlstor by carrying along the moistened layer of fresh snow from the middle of the week and pushed far down into the Flache Tälchen valley.

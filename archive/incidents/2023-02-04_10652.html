Description

A group of nine skiers was on the Hählekopf mountain. During the descent – they were skiing one at a time – the fourth person triggered an avalanche and was partially buried. They were able to free themselves and, like everyone else, remained unharmed. Source: written information with estimated key data submitted to the avalanche warning service by one of the participants.
